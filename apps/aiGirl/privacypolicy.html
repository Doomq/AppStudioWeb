Information Collection and Use

Luna: AI Companion does not collect any personal information. We do not gather names, email addresses, locations, device identifiers, or advertising IDs. Your conversations remain stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

This app uses RevenueCat solely to manage subscriptions and validate purchases. RevenueCat stores only anonymous purchase information required for your subscription and does not track or profile you.
