Luna: AI Companion — Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

The application does not collect any personal information from users. No names, email addresses, locations, or device identifiers are collected. The application does not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) and does not track users across apps or services.

Third-Party Services

The application uses RevenueCat solely to manage subscriptions and validate purchases. RevenueCat does not collect or process personal information for advertising, marketing, or behavioral profiling. RevenueCat’s data collection is limited to subscription status and anonymized purchase information required to manage your subscription.

Advertising

The application does not display any third-party advertisements or contextual advertising.

Children’s Privacy

The application fully complies with Apple’s Kids Category guidelines. The application does not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under the age of 13. If any such data is inadvertently collected, it will be promptly deleted.

Data Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Only anonymized subscription purchase data (non-personal) is stored via RevenueCat for as long as necessary to manage subscriptions.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect all non-personal data stored within the app.
