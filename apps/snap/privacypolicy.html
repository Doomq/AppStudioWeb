Snap to PDF — Privacy Policy

Overview

Snap to PDF is a simple image‑to‑PDF and document scanning app. We designed it to respect your privacy: we do not create user accounts, and we do not collect personal information for analytics or advertising.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect personal information such as your name, email, address, or device identifiers (including IDFA). Your scans, photos, and generated PDFs are processed on-device using Apple frameworks (e.g., VisionKit, PDFKit) and remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Permissions We Request

  • Camera — to capture documents using the device camera.
  • Photos — to import images from your photo library and to optionally save exported PDFs to Photos.
  • Files / iCloud Drive — to save or export PDFs to the Files app or iCloud Drive when you choose to do so.

Permissions are used solely for the stated functionality. You can change these permissions in your device Settings at any time.

User Content

“User Content” includes photos, scans, PDFs, and file names you create in the app. This content is stored locally on your device. You control export and deletion. Temporary processing files may be created during export and are removed by the app or the system.

Third‑Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage in‑app purchases (subscriptions and lifetime unlock). RevenueCat processes anonymized purchase receipts and subscription status to validate your entitlement. It does not use your data for advertising or behavioral profiling.

When you share or export a file via the iOS Share Sheet to another service (e.g., Mail, Drive, Dropbox), that action is initiated by you, and the destination service’s privacy policy applies.

Advertising

The app does not display third‑party ads and does not share data for advertising or tracking purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Purchase metadata required for entitlement validation may be shared with RevenueCat. No analytics SDKs or advertising networks are integrated.

Data Retention

RevenueCat retains non‑personal purchase records for as long as necessary to provide your subscription entitlements and comply with legal obligations. Your locally stored User Content remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Security

We rely on iOS sandboxing and system protections to help safeguard your locally stored content. While no system can be 100% secure, we do not transmit your documents to our servers, reducing exposure risk.

Children’s Privacy

Snap to PDF is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at main@jellyapps.cz.
