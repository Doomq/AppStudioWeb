Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

Information Collection and Use

Buddy: AI Friend does not collect any personal information. We do not gather names, email addresses, locations, device identifiers, or advertising IDs. Your conversations are stored only locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Third-Party Services

This app uses RevenueCat solely to manage subscriptions and verify purchases. RevenueCat does not collect any personal data for advertising or profiling. It only stores anonymous purchase information needed to track your subscription status.

Advertising

No ads are displayed in the app, and no advertising or tracking technologies are used.

Children’s Privacy

This app complies with Apple’s Kids Category guidelines. It does not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children under the age of 13. If any such data is discovered, it will be permanently deleted.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

Data Retention

Only anonymized, non-personal subscription data is retained via RevenueCat for as long as needed to manage your subscription. No personal data is stored or retained.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect all anonymized data handled by third-party services and ensure it is used only for subscription management.
